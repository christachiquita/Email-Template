DIRECTIONS TO THE HOTEL

If you are coming from the North via the Sydney Harbour Bridge, take the exit towards the city from the Western Distributor. Continue along the Western Distributor and turn onto Sussex St. Turn onto Little Hay St, turn again onto Harbour St and then you will reach Little Pier St where you will find the hotel.

PLEASE be aware of Sydney’s ongoing Light Rail Construction Works all along George Street

If you are coming in from Ultimo Road, please turn onto George St. From George St please turn left onto Goulburn St and then left again onto Sussex St. From Sussex you can turn right onto Little Hay and turn right again onto Harbour St leading onto Little Pier St

 

If you are coming from Sydney Airport, follow Ross Smith Avenue to General Holmes Drive. Take Botany Road via Redfern to George Street, then left into Goulburn, left into  Sussex Street, continue down to Harbour Street via Hay Street  to reach Little Pier Street.

Refurbishment FAQ’s
Question: How much noise will there be?
Answer: In an effort to minimize the impact on our guests work will commence from 9am and cease at 5pm from Monday – Saturday, with no work being carried out on Sunday.

Question: Will there be work on my floor?
Answer: No. We will be closing off an entire floor at a time starting from the top floor and working our way down.

Question: Will the lobby be under construction when I check in?
Answer: The Lobby and Ceezens restaurant will only be under construction at the very end of the refurbishment from Mid October until December.

Question: What work is being done?
Answer: In simple terms, everything. We are building brand new rooms, conference rooms, restaurant, lobby and lobby bar.

Question: Can I still have breakfast, lunch & dinner?
Answer: Absolutely. For breakfast our Ceezens restaurant will be open until October and then we will relocate our breakfast whilst Ceezens undergoes its refurbishment. For lunch and dinner you will always be able to access the Pumphouse Bar.

Question: Will there still be lift access?
Answer: Yes, we will always have a lift operating throughout the refurbishment. Please note we will be refurbishing our lifts as well so we will have only have 1 available from mid June.

Question: How long will the hotel be under construction?
Answer: Our refurbishment commences on April 26th, 2017 and will finish mid December, 2017

Question: Will you still have car parking?
Answer: We do have very limited spaces available here at the hotel. The closest car parking is at Willson’s Car Park Darling Quarter. Please call the hotel if you require any directions.

Question: What do I do if there is a problem during my stay?
Answer: Please see or call the Duty Manager at the front desk, as we will do our utmost to ensure you have a comfortable stay.
